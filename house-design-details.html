Compact design and practicality

With its compact design and great use of space, the Aldinga is sure to present the growing family with a fantastic living arrangement. With a private master bedroom, featuring its own ensuite and walk-in-robe, this great house design also contains two other good sized bedrooms as well as a large open plan living area towards the back of the home. With a sliding door off the kitchen and family room, you can also step outside into a great Australian backyard. A choice of attractive facades completes this compact home.

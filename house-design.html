Home Designs To Help You Love Where You Live

The huge range of home designs available at Metricon means you are spoilt for choice for your new home build or house & land package. We want you to love where you live and we work with you every step of the way to achieve your goals. Our multiple award-winning house designs are just the start of your journey with Metricon. We will also personalise your project with outstanding features, fittings, inclusions, and a wide variety of layouts.

With Metricon, you are assured that your builder is highly qualified with more than 45 years of home building experience. We are custom home specialists with the ability to create quality homes at affordable prices for Australians. If you are looking to upgrade your lifestyle, choose Metricon.

Choose a home builder that's perfect for Adelaide

When you choose Metricon to build your dream home, you're choosing a home that is stylish, affordable, and built for South Australian families. Whether you're after a single or double-storey house, our Adelaide home designs prioritise natural light for spacious and contemporary living. At Metricon, we build high-quality affordable homes that help you love where you live, and it's easy to see why so many people love the South Australian lifestyle.

Metricon's home designs suit the unique South Australian landscape, and are perfect for almost any block of land. For example, if yours is a sloping block, we can create a high quality split level home that is sure to be the envy of your neighbours. Whether you live in Mount Barker, the Adelaide Hills or North, South or West of Adelaide or, Metricon has the ideal home for you and your family.

Visit our display villages to find out more

Visiting one of Metricon's display homes across Adelaide, South Australia, is a great way to get inspired about your new home ideas. Take time to explore the layouts and feel of several Metricon homes. Our display village family homes give you the opportunity to experience up close the high quality features and fittings of our home designs.

Click on any of our designs to see comprehensive floor plan details as well as the inclusions that come with the advertised price. When you visit a display centre, take time to speak with our expert sales staff and ask questions about the Metricon building process and your potential to own the home of your dreams. If you can't make it in person, enjoy the experience with a virtual tour from the comfort of home.

Why choose Metricon new home designs?
